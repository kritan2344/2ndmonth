Two months time passed so fast
But I really hope we together last
Seeing you the first time
The first feeling I had, is the same yet still it shines.
Holding your hands sitting on a river side
Talk to each other may our bond abide

You faked me
You told things never last
See me, love in me, has even been more,
Talk to you, gaze the moon, with you on a river shore

60 days, 1440 hours 86400 minutes we were together,
If I ever get blesssed I will ask may we both get a death waiver
May your eyes always shine me
Being sure, mine will always shine you and forever it will be

May every version of ours in every universe are together
Because, if not I now know how much of me would have suffer
Seeing you, picturing you in every love reels
A glimpse to your picture, heaven is what my heart and mind feels.
